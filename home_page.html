About Our Center

Welcome to Abhishek Computer Center

Located in the heart of Latifpur Urf Sipahiwala, Seohara, Bijnor, we are committed to providing quality digital education to empower our students with essential computer skills for the modern world.

Our newly established center in June 2025 brings together experienced instructors and state-of-the-art facilities to create an ideal learning environment.

Our Mission:

  • To make digital education accessible to everyone in our community
  • To provide practical, job-oriented computer training
  • To bridge the digital divide in our region
  • To foster a culture of continuous learning in technology
